Privacy Support

Local release-candidate draft

Privacy should be as clear as the battlefield.

This notice describes the current offline Siege Winds candidate. It is a working draft, has not been deployed, and must be reviewed against the exact final app binary before publication.

Effective date: 18 July 2026 (draft)

Bundle identifier: com.siegewinds.game

Expected public URL after approval: https://siegewinds.com/privacy

Not a final public policy

The public developer identity and postal address below are placeholders. Store privacy answers, live-service configuration, legal review, and a frozen-binary inspection are still required.

What the current offline candidate does

  • Stores game progress and settings locally on the player’s device.
  • Does not require an online account to play Tutorial, Solo, Campaign, Master Circuit, or Local 1v1.
  • Keeps Unity Gaming Services disabled, so async matches and online rankings are not active.
  • Does not intentionally request or display an advertisement while the runtime ad gate remains disabled.

Local app storage may include progress, unlocks, in-game balances, equipped cannon and fortress choices, records, preferences, checkpoints, local match state, and a randomly generated local guest identifier. Siege Winds does not receive this local data in the current offline configuration. The operating system may include it in a device backup under the player’s Apple or Google backup settings.

Important embedded SDK disclosure

Google Mobile Ads for Unity and Google’s User Messaging Platform (GMA and UMP) are currently embedded in the candidate, but disabled at runtime. The candidate uses test app identifiers, blank interstitial unit identifiers, and a disabled ad gate. No ad request is intended in this offline configuration.

GMA and UMP are embedded but disabled. Before publication, the release owner must either exclude them from the binary and re-verify its composition, or finish their live configuration and update this notice, consent flow, and both stores’ advertising and privacy disclosures to match the exact binary.

Purchases

Unity IAP is embedded in the current candidate and is designed to initialize only after a player chooses a purchase or restore action. Live products and store-channel purchase or restore behavior are not yet proven. If purchases are included in the published binary, Apple or Google processes the transaction under its own terms. Siege Winds does not receive the player’s full payment-card details.

Information sent for support

If a player emails us, we receive the email address, message, and any files the player chooses to attach. We use that information to answer the request, investigate the reported issue, and keep a reasonable support record. Players should not send passwords, payment-card details, government identifiers, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

Support and privacy messages are handled through Apple iCloud Mail. A public retention period and deletion process must be approved before deployment; until then, this draft makes no unsupported retention promise.

Sharing and sale

The offline app does not sell personal information. Information is shared only as needed with the platform store for an optional purchase, with the email provider to answer a message, with the website host to serve and secure these pages, or with authorities and advisers when the law or safety requires it. This section must be expanded if any online, advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting service becomes active.

This static website package

These draft pages contain no analytics, advertising, external scripts, cookie banners, tracking pixels, or third-party web fonts. They do not set cookies. A future host must not add tracking without first updating the public notice and obtaining any consent required by law.

Player choices and deletion

In the current offline candidate, progress remains on the device. A player can remove the local app data through the device’s app-management controls or by uninstalling the app. If online accounts or services are enabled later, this section must be replaced with the verified account, access, correction, objection, and deletion process for those services.

A player may ask to access, correct, or delete information they sent by email, subject to applicable law, by contacting privacy@siegewinds.com.

Children

The current offline candidate does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app. If a parent or guardian believes a child sent personal information by email, they can contact the privacy address below. The final wording and audience declaration must match the approved Apple and Google age-rating questionnaires.

Security and changes

Reasonable safeguards should match the limited information handled, but no storage or transmission method is completely secure. If the app or its services change, the deployed notice must be updated and its public “Last updated” date changed before the affected release is submitted.
