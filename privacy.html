Website privacy

A clear record of this site.

No signup. No ad tracking. No hidden form. This page explains the privacy status of the Siege Winds coming-soon website while the mobile game remains unreleased.

Updated 22 July 2026

Coming-soon website

This page covers the public website, not the unreleased mobile game.

No public contact form or support inbox is presented yet.

Scope of this notice Siege Winds is still in development. This status page is not the mobile app’s privacy policy. That policy will be published only after the release build, services, public legal identity, and contact process are verified.

01

What this website does

The coming-soon site is made of static pages. It has no account, newsletter form, contact form, analytics service, advertising technology, tracking pixel, or third-party script.

  • The Siege Winds page code does not set cookies.
  • It does not ask for a name, phone number, location, or payment details.
  • There is no public data-entry or support-request field on this site.

02

Technical delivery data

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like other web hosts, its infrastructure may process standard connection information—such as an IP address, browser or device details, the requested page, and request time—to deliver and secure the site.

That hosting activity is governed by the GitHub General Privacy Statement. GitHub also describes the cookies and similar technologies that may apply to its services. Siege Winds does not add its own analytics or advertising measurement to these pages.

03

Before the game is released

The app privacy policy will identify the verified responsible party and describe the exact public build, including online play, player identity, purchases, advertising, consent, analytics, or crash reporting if any of them are enabled.

A public support or privacy contact will appear only after the address and handling process are verified. If this website adds a form, analytics, or another data service, this page will be updated before that change goes live.
