01

What is Siege Winds?

A tactical artillery duel built around one clear objective: breach the rival fortress and destroy its exposed core before your own keep falls.

You choose the target, set the cannon angle and powder, then fire. Wind affects the trajectory, but it is one battlefield condition—not the purpose of the game.
