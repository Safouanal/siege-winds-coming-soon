Local release-candidate draft

Help for the next shot.

This page covers the current offline Siege Winds candidate. It is a draft, has not been deployed, and is not evidence that either store release is ready.

Release identity: version 1.0.0, build 135 candidate.

Expected public URL after approval: https://siegewinds.com/support

Current candidate boundary

Tutorial, Solo, Campaign, Master Circuit, and same-device Local 1v1 are the reviewable offline paths. Async multiplayer, online rankings, live ads, and live store purchases have not completed production-service and store-channel verification.

If the game does not behave as expected

  1. Close and reopen the game, then repeat the shortest path that caused the issue.
  2. Check that the device has free storage and a supported, current operating-system update.
  3. Record the app version, platform, device model, operating-system version, and exact on-screen message.
  4. Email support with the steps that reproduce the issue and a screenshot if it is safe to share.

Do not include passwords, payment-card details, government identifiers, or other unnecessary sensitive information in a support request.

The target minimums are iOS 15 and Android 7.1 (API 25). Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app can erase local progress; a platform backup may restore it according to Apple or Google settings.

Purchase help

Live products and store-channel purchase or restore flows are not yet proven for this candidate. If the final release offers a purchase, payment is handled by Apple or Google. Never email payment-card details; include only the non-sensitive store order reference needed for support.

Ads, consent, and online services

Google Mobile Ads for Unity and Google’s User Messaging Platform (GMA and UMP) are currently embedded but disabled. Before publication, they must either be excluded and the binary re-verified, or fully configured, tested, and reflected in the consent flow, privacy notice, and store disclosures. The current offline candidate is not a live-ad test.

Unity Gaming Services remains disabled in this candidate. Therefore async matches and online rankings should not be presented as available until the backend and two-account sandbox evidence are complete.
